# Products

Products are the core of LicenseDock. Each product represents a digital item you sell – a Joomla extension, a desktop app, a theme, etc.

## Product Structure

LicenseDock uses a three-level hierarchy:

```
Product → Plan → Price
```

- **Product** – what you're selling (e.g., "My Joomla Extension")
- **Plan** – a tier or variant (e.g., "Single Site", "Developer", "Agency")
- **Price** – a billing option on a plan (e.g., "$49/year", "$99 one-time")

A product can have multiple plans, and each plan can have multiple prices with different billing cycles.

## Creating a Product

1. Go to **Components → LicenseDock → Products**
2. Click **New**
3. Fill in:
   - **Name** – product name shown to customers
   - **Slug** – the product name as it appears in the URL (auto-generated from the name)
   - **Description** – full product description (shown on product page)
   - **Status** – Published or Unpublished
4. Save

## Product Settings

- **Image** – product image for the product page and listings
- **Downloads** – attach downloadable files to the product
- **Tags** – categorize products for filtering
