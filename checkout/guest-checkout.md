# Guest Checkout

LicenseDock supports guest checkout, allowing customers to purchase without having an account first.

## How It Works

1. Guest clicks **Buy Now** on a product page
2. Checkout page shows a login/register form
3. Guest can either:
   - **Log in** with an existing account
   - **Register** a new account
4. After logging in or registering, checkout continues with their selected product

LicenseDock remembers what they were buying through the login/registration process, so they don't have to start over.
